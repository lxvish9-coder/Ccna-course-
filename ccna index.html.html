No — Module 1 starts from absolute basics. If you know how to use a computer, you're ready. The course is designed to take complete beginners through to exam-ready in around 3 months at a part-time pace.

This course is aligned with the CCNA 200-301 exam, the current version as of 2024. Cisco updates the exam blueprint periodically and we update the course content to match within 30 days of any changes.

Yes. Cisco Packet Tracer is completely free. We include a setup guide to get it running on Windows, Mac, or Linux. All lab files are provided as downloadable .pkt files you can open directly.
